# Business Model

---

## Revenue Stream 1: Premium Subscription

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | Basic context matching (time + movement), 5 context switches/day, limited library |
| **Premium** | $9.99-14.99/mo | Full sensor suite, unlimited awareness, recurring motifs, place themes, Moment Replay, character profiles, Blend Mode, themed modes |

---

## Revenue Stream 2: Platform Partnership Revenue

Streaming platforms pay per-user referral fee or rev share for:

- Premium conversions driven by Underscore (free users → paid to avoid ads during boss fights)
- Increased engagement hours (4-6x normal streaming time)
- Reduced churn among Underscore users

---

## Revenue Stream 3: Themed Experiences

Game studios and film studios license official "modes":

- **Kojima Productions:** Official MGS Life Soundtrack mode ($4.99)
- **Square Enix:** Final Fantasy mode (commute = world map, office = dungeon, clocking out = victory fanfare)
- **Capcom:** Devil May Cry mode
- **FromSoftware:** Mode that plays ominous music near doorways

---

## Revenue Stream 4: The Emotional Map (Long-Term)

Aggregated, anonymized behavioral data about human emotional patterns. When do people feel energized vs depleted? What music actually alleviates anxiety? Research-grade data for wellness companies, academic institutions, mental health orgs. ONLY with explicit consent and privacy-first architecture.

---

## Why Streaming Platforms Would Fight For This

### Spotify
Underscore users stream 4-6x more hours. Churn drops to near zero. Free users convert to Premium IMMEDIATELY (ads during boss fights = UNACCEPTABLE). Deep catalog tracks get discovered.

### Apple Music
Underscore is the missing piece of the Apple ecosystem. iPhone sensors + Apple Watch heart rate + AirPods + Apple Music = complete life soundtrack experience. WWDC keynote material.

### YouTube Music
Deepest catalog (includes game OSTs, anime OSTs, fan remixes, specific live performances). Underscore + YouTube Music = the ULTIMATE library for the anime/gaming audience.
